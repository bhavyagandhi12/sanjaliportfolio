Overview

An application for every NMIMS student to keep-up with what’s happening on campus, track their attendance, classes. The one-stop for your student life.

  • Research: Target users & aim of the application
  • Discover: Problem Statement & Solution
  • Insight : Features of the app
  • Design: High Fidelity Designs

Research - Target Users & Aim of the App

  • Target Users:
    Current students: to look at their class schedules, assignments due, events happening on campus, keep their attendance in check, etc.
    Faculty/Professors: to upload notes, grade assignments all in one app


  • Aim of the App:
    • Most students spend their days in university trying to make the most of their experience on campus.
    NMIMS App aims at maximising this experience for students by highlighting all the helpful, essential details that a student could benefit from.

Discover - Problem statement and solution

Discovering the Problem

  • Lack of Interactive Sources: The current portal doesn’t offer any interactive resources that could help students directly
  • Accessibility: To access basic information, students need to login into a web portal instead of having the option to access it easily, from an app that is easier to access
  • Social life:Events happening on campus are spread through word of mouth and bulletin boards, instead of an efficient method being employed

Defining a Solution

  • Interactive Sources: An app could be made for students to manage their life at university. From classes to extracurriculars, this app could be a resource for students to use readily.
  • Accessibility:Instead of having a web portal, an app is easier to access and more widely used by the student population. Having a personalised app which displays information relevant to the student would prove to be useful.
  • Social life:Keeping up with social events on campus, offered by various clubs could be easily communicated by the help of the app, acting as a student union.

Features of the application

  • Personalised login: students can login using this SAP ID (unique roll number) for easier access to the classes they are taking in a particular semester
  • Clubs and committees: With the aim to help students with their social life, a page dedicated to each club on campus, explaining what they do, how to join, events they have conducted, etc
