i4G x Zuri Scholarship

Overview

Ingressive for Good (i4G) is a non-profit organisation based out of Africa, that aims to help students break into technology.

Zuri is an organisation based out of Oregon, that helps students scale their career in technology with the help of online taught, task-based courses, called the Zuri Scholarship. It is a 3 month scholarship program. Selected top performers that meet a certain criteria proceed to the stage of live implementation thereby graduating.

I was selected for the Summer Cohort Scholarship Program of 2022, under the full time Product Design Track, being one of the few students selected from my country, India.

The following are the tasks i completed as part of my training:
  • Task 1: Laws of UX
  • Task 2: Design Thinking task
  • Task 3: Re-designing task
  • Task 4: Creating a Style Guide
  • Task 5: User Research

Task 1: Laws of UX

Assignment A - Digital product
Review the apps and websites below; Identify which UX laws they got right and which ones they got wrong. Attach pictures supporting your answers.
1. Nigeria railway website;
2. Nigerian Railway App;
3. African Union Website;
4. Organization of African Petroleum Exporting Countries;

Assignment B - Physical product Review one physical product and state the laws of UX followed and the ones not followed.Attach a picture of the product that you reviewed supporting your answer. Example of a physical product: powerbank, traffic light, etc

Task 4: Creating a Style Guide

Create a style guide that consists of the following:
Color system (Pick a color then generate styles from the tints and shades of that color)
Typography (Choose a font and create a typography scale.)
Iconography (Find an icon pack from the Figma community, duplicate and copy it into your Figma file)
Spacing system (Create a spacing standard)
Grid system (Create a grid system for a Desktop, tablet, and mobile screens)
Buttons system (All buttons should be auto-layout components and variants)
Input fields (Should consist of different states)
